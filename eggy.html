EggyDrop

eat happy, eat clean.

Background

Web Design: Strategy and Information Systems

Instructor: Roman Jaster & Michael Worthington

Duration: 4 weeks

Role: Solo UX/UI Designer

Restaurant Concept

Restaurant Name & URL:

    Restaurant Name: Eggy Drop
    Potential URL: eggydropsandwiches.com

Food & Drink:

    • Famous Korean-style egg drop sandwiches with varying ingredients: Simple Egg Sandwich, Ham & Cheese Sandwich, and Bulgogi Sandwich
    • Dessert Sandwiches: Omelet bbang in either strawberry, Oreo, or mango flavors
    • Drinks: Dalgoma Coffee, Cafe Latte, Matcha Latte, Dirty Matcha, lemonade, black milk tea, jasmine green milk tea, milk, water

Cost:

    In order to be cost-efficient yet still affordable for the general public, these sandwiches will cost 6.5 dollars each.

Location:

    Will be United States based, in California, working with big cities such as those found in Los Angeles, Orange County, or San Francisco.

Elevator Pitch:

    Our fluffy cloud-like egg sandwiches bring the taste of Korea straight to your door. Whether you want a filling savory meal or a sweet dessert, we meet all your tastebuds needs.

Target Audience

Role: Students, Foodies, Social Media Fanatics

Demographics:

    • Age: young adults & teens (15-25)
    • Location: living in/near city
    • Income: moderate-low income
    • Marital Status: single
    • Occupation: high school and college students

Psycholographics:

    • Personality & Attitudes: Love aesthetics, love food, patient, interested in social media
    • Values: adventurous
    • Lifestyles: interested in trying new things

User Needs:

    • Customizable ingredients
    • multiple food options
    • decent price
    • food delivery options
    • easy to navigate

Client Needs:

    • Easy navigation of site
    • aesthetic branding
    • promotion of new products
    • multiple food options
    • provides a quick food delivery service

User Personas

Outline of Scope

Content Requirements

  • A menu of items with images of each
    • Egg Sandwiches (ham & cheese, only egg, bulgogi)
    • Desserts (strawberry, mango, oreo)
    • Drinks (coffees, teas)
  • Add or remove buttons to show available ingredients and allow customization
  • Track order status
    • Progress bar to show if the order is being prepared, cooked, delivered
    • Map to show where it is
  • Checkout options
    • Payment area where card is accepted or online payment services
    • Tip can be added here as well
  • Confirmation
    • Once order is placed or any action is taken a confirmation action should take place to allow people to know they actually fulfilled their action

Functional Requirements

  • Place a food order
    • Make payment
  • Customize food ingredients
    • Swap ingredients in and out or get pre-made options
  • Add Delivery Details
    • Select between pickup or delivery
    • Add tip (custom amount or suggested amounts)
  • Cancel order
    • Delete items in the cart
    • Refund money
  • Track order
