Problem Statement

GameIn aims to provide an established integrated team find, sponsorship, certification, and transfer market platform in the gaming and eSports industry. The vision for the platform strives to define GameIn as a leading matchmaking system between eSports teams and sponsorship organizations. It serves as a "one-stop-shop" for endemic and non-endemic stakeholders looking for teams, influencers, athletes, coaches, partners, and investment opportunities in order to improve their business and achieve strategic goals.

As a development team under GameIn, our purpose is to enhance and further the platform's existing user experience and user interface through various combined skill sets, ultimately shaping the platform into an even more engaging and interactive system.

Reflection

Key Takeaways

1. Communication is key!

In order for everyone to be on the same page and keep our progress steady, our team was constantly updating each other on our own individual progress as well as what each person would do next. In addition, we set up weekly meetings with our sponsor to update them with what we had done so far and give an opportunity for feedback on our designs. This feedback really communicated to me what the intentions of our sponsor was and how to properly achieve their vision for GameIn.

2. Be Adaptable

During this internship, I worked on a project in the earlier stages of developement so many ideas were not concrete yet. We went through various low-mid fidelity designs showcasing possible features and oftentimes had a lot of changes to make during feedback sessions. This really proved the importance of feedback as well as wireframes, since the changes would have been much more difficult in higher fidelity mockups. Overall, this feeback process gave a lot of insight on what the future of GameIn would look like so it was important to be attentive to the changes asked by the Sponsor.
