Qtea

Boba runs made easy.

Background

Visual Elements of User Interface Design Course 2022

Instructor: Michael Worthington & Roman Jaster

Duration: 4 weeks

Role: Solo UI Designer

Project Overview

Who: An app to help people find their local boba shops at any time of day

What: Primarily for people who cannot drive or do no want to travel far for boba

Where: Smartphone or tablet

Description: Need a boba spot fast? Can't drive yet? Qtea helps users find nearby boba spots when theyâ€™re short on time, taking a study break, or just want to have fun with friends. Qtea takes in user location and matches it with nearby boba spots; users can specify the distance they want to travel and the search will adjust accordingly to fit their updated preferences. The distance of shops, the availability (wait time), and the time of day (checks what is open at the given time of day) is all taken into account when matching users with their best preferences!

Moodboard

To begin the brainstorming process of what the app should look like. I put together a moodboard consisting of 15 different images, a 5 color pallete, and a sample typography. Each of these elements hold a uniqueness to them while still being cohesive in the grander image.
