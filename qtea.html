Qtea.

An app to help people find their local boba spots quickly and easily.

Duration: 4 weeks

Role: UI Designer

Team: Individual Project

Tools: Figma, Google Suite, Adobe XD

Timeline

Project Brief

An app that finds a specific food within a given area

The app topic is simple so that the final product can be contained within two screens. In order to create a functioning app, it was crucial to fully optimize these two main pages with clear interfaces and strategically organized content.

Project Overview

People want to find local boba shops without a lot of hassle.

Who: Primarily for people who cannot drive or do no want to travel far for boba

What: An app to help people find their local boba shops at any time of day

Where: Smartphone or tablet

Description: Need a boba spot fast? Can't drive yet? Qtea helps users find nearby boba spots when they're short on time, taking a study break, or just want to have fun with friends. Qtea takes in user location and matches it with nearby boba spots; users can specify the distance they want to travel and the search will adjust accordingly to fit their updated preferences. The distance of shops, the availability (wait time), and the time of day (checks what is open at the given time of day) is all taken into account when matching users with their best preferences!

Explore Page

This page is the landing page where users first arrive. There are features to help search for boba shops such as the filter, the search bar, and the recommended options.

Map

The map allows users to visually see the difference between them and the surrounding boba shops. There is also a list that can be scrolled through, sorted by closest to farthest.
